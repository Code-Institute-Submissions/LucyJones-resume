My story

I’ve always loved the feeling of learning something new, challenging myself and seeing results.

When I was put on furlough during the COVID pandemic I saw this as the perfect opportunity to develop a new skill set that would be valued in the future working world. My decision was coding.

My next questions were then;

“What specifically do I want to learn?”

and

“Where do I want to learn it?”

After doing some research and completing taster courses through different learning providers, I decided Code Institute was the right starting platform for me.

I am excited to begin my journey and continuously learn as I build my portfolio to boost my career as a full stack web developer.
